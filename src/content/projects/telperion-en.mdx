## The Problem

Telperion is an NGO focused on climate education, but their previous website was visually broken, difficult to navigate, and lacked basic accessibility features. This made their crucial resources almost invisible online and hindered their mission to educate the public.

## The Approach

I rebuilt the platform from the ground up using Astro for fast static generation and a minimal JavaScript footprint. The new architecture is fully bilingual (English and Czech), adheres strictly to WCAG accessibility guidelines, and is styled with a modular Tailwind CSS system. I prioritized performance and resilience, ensuring the site loads instantly even on slow connections.

## The Outcome

The result is a sustainable, lightning-fast website that Telperion fully owns. I also delivered comprehensive architectural documentation, empowering future contributors to maintain and extend the site without friction or dependency on any single developer.
